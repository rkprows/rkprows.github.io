Snowbuddies

For skiiers and snowboarders who don't want to head up the mountain alone, Snowbuddies is a planning tool, connecting users to one another based on resort, date, time, and skill level. Users fill out a profile, and post about upcoming trips they would like to take including where they are going, their skill level, and where they want to meet.

Snowbuddies was built is a Rails app with Bootstrap styling and a postgresql database and one 2nd at a local hackathon.

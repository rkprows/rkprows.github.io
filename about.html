about

In West Philadelphia, born and raised … oh wait, wrong story.
Hi, I'm Rachel. I was actually born and raised in Northbrook, IL (a suburb of Chicago). I left the Windy City to attend the University of Utah in Salt Lake City and later graduated from Utah Valley University with a B.A. in History. Right up until graduation, I planned on teaching high school history. However, graduation loomed, that plan no longer seemed to fit and I was left wondering What next? I tried my hand at interior design and event planning while I figured out what I really wanted in a career. About a year and a half into my job planning events I was asked to create a registration website for some upcoming events. I had zero development skills and ended up using a template to get the job done. While it met our needs, it was a little clunky and certainly wasn't what I would have created on my own. It got me thinking, What if I could do this the way I want? I started reading everything I could and using any free online resources I could find on building websites. From there I decided the tech industry, specifically web development, was where I wanted to be. After some research I decided to quit my job and attend a three-month intensive development boot camp, through DevPoint Labs, here in Salt Lake City, UT. Thus began my love affair with web development. The more I learn the harder I fall. I love that this is an ever-changing industry that requires consistent learning to stay relevant. I find great satisfaction in the juxtaposition of the technical skills and creativity that exists in web development.
